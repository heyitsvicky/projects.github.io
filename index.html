Technoblade

Technoblade is a minecraft youtuber who was part of a minecraft roleplay server (the Dream SMP). Yes maybe it sounds like a strange interest but I swear, his character was written incredibly well (as were the other characters). It's important to note his character is an anarchist and he hears voices that ask for blood. Technoblade's character, nicknamed "The Blade", is a piglin hybrid which makes him very strong. As such, Techno is often depicted with a crown, cape, and tusks. The stoyline is basically about him trying to help his family but ending up betrayed in the end. He is very strong and powerful and his catch phrase is "Technoblade never dies" for a good reason as no one has been able to kill him. Note: Other important characters are Phil (depicted with a bucket hat and wings), Tommy and Tubbo (depicted as children)
